How do I Find my USAA user ID for online banking?


If you’ve forgotten your USAA user ID for online banking, don’t worry—it’s easy to retrieve. Simply visit usaa.com/verify and follow the steps to verify your identity. You’ll need your USAA number or Social Security Number and a few personal details to confirm your account. Once verified, your user ID will be displayed or sent to your registered email. This secure process ensures quick access to your USAA online banking.

Steps to Find Your USAA User ID

Follow these simple steps to recover your USAA user ID:

  • Visit the Official USAA Website
    • Open your web browser and go to usaa.com/verify.
  • Click on “Log On”
    • In the top-right corner of the homepage, click the “Log On” button.
    • Under the login fields, click the “Forgot ID?” link.
  • Verify Your Identity
    • You’ll be prompted to enter personal information such as:
      • Your USAA number or Social Security Number (SSN)
      • Date of birth
    • Click “Next” to continue.
  • Follow the On-Screen Instructions
    • After verifying your identity, USAA will provide your user ID or allow you to create a new one if necessary.
  • Access Your Account
    • Use the recovered user ID to log in to your USAA online banking account.

Tips for Remembering Your User ID

  • Save your user ID in a secure password manager.
  • Create a user ID that’s easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.
  • Avoid writing it down where others can access it.
